Description

The purpose of this game is for the user to dodge different obstacles(sea mines) while operating a submarine. There is no end of the game. The objective is to dodge as long as possible and earn more points before getting destroyed. The speed of the mines coming at the sub increases over time I decided to make a project like this because I am interested in the navy and armed forces in general. I also play a lot of war games and have a lot of models, some of which with lego, that are themed to the military and the armed forces. I decided to make this to show my own interests in this type of game and to see what interesting things that I could make with the help of an LLM

Instructions

The user controls the movement of the submarine wit the arrow keys of their computer. They use these controls to dodge the incoming mines and to survive for as long as possible. Up arrow- Moves the submarine up near the surface to dodge the mines that are coming at it and allows the user to move the sub to a safer spot near the top of the screen Down Arrow- It moves the submarine down near the bottom to dodge any mines that are coming straight at it to go to an open position available at the bottom of the screen Left arrow- It is used to go backwards so that you can go back to an empty position that the player could have missed before Right arrow- The arrow moves the submarine forward. It could be used for going forward to go to any available space that they have
